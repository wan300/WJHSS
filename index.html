About the Journal

World Journal of Young and Excellent Scholars（WJYES）is an international, peer-reviewed, interdisciplinary journal dedicated to showcasing outstanding research, creative thinking, and innovative projects conducted by high school students around the world. The mission of the journal is to provide a global platform for young scholars to share their academic and intellectual achievements across a wide range of disciplines. WJYES welcomes submissions from all subject areas including science, technology, engineering, mathematics, humanities, social sciences, and the arts. The journal aims to inspire academic excellence and foster early research experiences among pre-university students. The journal is a Gold Open Access journal, and all articles are freely available to online readers without any subscription or access fees.

Welcome submissions from young scholars！

June 5, 2025

We warmly welcome submissions from high school students across the globe, regardless of background or location. WJYES is open to all disciplines—including science, technology, engineering, mathematics—and actively encourages cross-disciplinary insights, experimental approaches, and novel methodologies.

Our mission is not only to publish exceptional student work, but also to ignite a lifelong passion for discovery, academic rigor, and scholarly communication. By engaging students in real-world publishing experiences early in their academic journey, we aim to cultivate the next generation of researchers, innovators, and thought leaders.

WJYES is an Open Access journal. All articles are freely available online, with no subscription, access fees, or paywalls—ensuring global visibility and impact for every published work.

Whether you're conducting experiments in a lab, analyzing literature or tackling social issues through research, WJYES is your stage to be heard on a global scale. We invite you to contribute, collaborate, and inspire.
