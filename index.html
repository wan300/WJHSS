About the Journal

World Journal of High School Students (WJHSS) is an international, peer-reviewed, interdisciplinary journal dedicated to showcasing outstanding research, creative thinking, and innovative projects conducted by high school students around the world. The mission of the journal is to provide a global platform for young scholars to share their academic and intellectual achievements across a wide range of disciplines. WJHSS welcomes submissions from all subject areas including science, technology, engineering, mathematics, humanities, social sciences, and the arts. The journal aims to inspire academic excellence and foster early research experiences among pre-university students. The journal is a Gold Open Access journal, and all articles are freely available to online readers without any subscription or access fees.
